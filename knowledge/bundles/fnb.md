# Food & Beverage (F&B) Vertical Bundle

## Market Context
- Extremely low margin, highly operational.
- Pain is measured in seconds (speed of service) and waste (spoilage).
- High staff turnover, training must take less than 10 minutes.

## Persona Attributes
- **Owner/Manager**: Stressed, always on their feet, hates complicated screens.
- **Staff**: Minimum wage, doesn't care about "gamification" or "loyalty points".

## Common Anti-Patterns
- PM designs for iPad in a quiet office, ignoring greasy hands and bright lights.
- PM assumes staff has time to scan QR codes during a lunch rush.
