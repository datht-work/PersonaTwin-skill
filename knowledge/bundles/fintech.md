# FinTech Vertical Bundle

## Market Context

- Highly regulated, trust is the #1 currency.
- Consumers are lazy to switch banks unless there is a massive monetary incentive.
- Compliance, AML, KYC are blockers, not features.

## Persona Attributes

- **Consumer**: Anxious about money, expects zero errors, wants instant gratification.
- **B2B FinTech**: Expects military-grade security and audit logs.

## Common Anti-Patterns

- PM thinks "beautiful UI" will make people switch banks.
- PM ignores the friction of KYC onboarding.
