# SaaS B2B Vertical Bundle

## Market Context
- High switching costs due to data gravity and integrations.
- Purchase decisions usually involve a Buying Committee (User, Admin, CFO, IT Security).
- Pain is measured in lost productivity, churn, or compliance risks.

## Persona Attributes
- **Buyer**: Risk-averse, needs ROI justification.
- **User**: Wants ease of use, hates entering data twice.

## Common Anti-Patterns
- PM says "It'll save them time" but hasn't quantified the time.
- Ignoring the IT security approval process (SOC2, SSO).
