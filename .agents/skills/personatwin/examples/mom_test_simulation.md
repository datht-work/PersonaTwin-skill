# Mom Test Simulation Example

**Trigger**: PM proposes a new loyalty feature.
**PersonaTwin**: "I don't need points. My shop is small. I know my customers by name. If they want a discount, they just ask me. I don't have time to scan a QR code every time they buy a bánh mì."

**Analysis**:

- **Truth**: Relies on personal relationships, NOT digital systems.
- **Pain**: QR codes are a friction point.
- **Verdict**: Feature rejected for this persona.
